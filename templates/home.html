Street Artist

Hello World! I'm Kroma an street artist from Concepción, Chile. My painting technique is the stencil painted with spray paint, I almost always paint at night because it's "illegal". I hope you enjoy seeing my work.

What's the difference between Art and Vandalism? Which is the Genius or the Anarquist?

This is about becoming so famous until what you do becomes legal, generating such a great impact that the justice and morals of society have to be modified, just to be able to give a view from another perspective.
