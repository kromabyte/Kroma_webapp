A little story about Me...

Kroma is my nickname, it is the abbreviation of the word croma√±on in Spanish, cro-magnon in English. I started painting stencil in 2013 motivated mainly by boredom. I remember being in the summer of 2013 sitting on my sofa at home watching the simpsons on television, I remember being very bored and the simpsons episode was when banksy collaborated, in that episode I saw bart become a street artist, While I was slowly recognizing what street art consisted of and this artist called Bansky, until then unknown to me.

I was very impressed by Banksy's art in how busy satire and ironies were in his artwork, so I set out to start stenciling. firstly I started painting t-shirts with stencils but then I realized that it was not enough, I started to investigate more about this art, then I got to know blek le rat and his history, for some the father of the stencil. After that I started to make a little bigger stencils but I didn't know where to paint them, since painting them on the street walls is illegal. But I remember finding a movie about banksy, the movie was "exit through the gift shop." Seeing that movie inspired me to start going out onto the streets to paint. What was the worst that could happen to me? The first stencil I painted in the streets was the face of "American psycho" and at that moment I knew that this was for me, I remember the sensation of adrenaline and serotonin running through my body and that I was not going to stop, the feeling of being doing something illegal, vandalism for some art for others. and since then I have been gaining more experience and losing my fear. I think I started all this out of the pure need to express myself, to leave my mark in this world, to show that I am not one more in this world, to show the world who is KROMA.
